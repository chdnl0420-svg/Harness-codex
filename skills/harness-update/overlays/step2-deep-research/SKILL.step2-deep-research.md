## CRITICAL: Step 2 Deep Research Auto-Decision

The Step 2 auto-decision is not limited to category filling. In noask mode the default Step 2 path is:

`harness-plan` skill → six category assumptions → Step 2 research trigger check → `harness-deep-researcher` when needed → Open Questions → automatic approval after Codex review.

In ask mode the default Step 2 path is:

`harness-plan-ask` skill → six category answers → Step 2 research trigger check → `harness-deep-researcher` when needed → Open Questions → user approval branch.

This block overrides any older Step 2 mapping that says only "6 카테고리 합리적 가정 + Open Questions".
